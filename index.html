How It Works
01
Click Your Section on the Map

On StubHub, click your section directly on the seating map. The extension captures all live listings for that section in real time — no API keys, no manual input required.

02
Enter Row and Quantity

Input your row and the number of tickets you're listing. VKT Pricer targets comparable rows automatically — not just the cheapest ticket in the section.

03
AI Scrapes, Cleans, and Analyzes

The extension strips filter noise — removes the Recommended Tickets toggle, sets quantity to show all listings — then sends the clean data set to our AI for analysis.

04
Get Your Price with Full Reasoning

The AI returns a specific price for each of your three goals, plus a written explanation of why that number makes sense given the current market structure.

Three Pricing Goals
Price to Sell

Positions your listing to move first. Undercuts the section floor so you're the most competitive ticket on the market — built for sellers who need it gone fast.

Price for Margin

Anchored to the nearest comparable rows rather than the floor — capturing real value without racing to the bottom. Best when you can hold a few days.

Hold High

A ceiling-anchored price for sellers who believe demand will rise as the event approaches. High risk, high reward — backed by AI reasoning on current trajectory.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How does Victory Kay Tickets work?

Simply submit your unused tickets through our "Book Now" form. We handle all pricing, listing, customer service, and transfers across major secondary marketplaces.

What sports and events do you handle?

We work with tickets from all major sports leagues including MLB, NFL, NBA, NHL, MLS, and premium events.

How do you price my tickets?

We use real-time market data, opponent strength, game timing, weather conditions, and historical trends to set optimal prices.

What marketplaces do you list on?

We list simultaneously on StubHub, Vivid Seats, SeatGeek, Ticketmaster Resale, and other leading platforms.

How do I get paid?

Once your tickets sell, we send you a full sale summary. We operate on an 80/20 split — 80% goes directly to you.

What if my tickets don't sell?

There are no upfront costs or fees. If your tickets don't sell, you pay nothing.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard security measures and coordinate transfers directly through official team systems.

Can I still use my tickets after submitting them?

Yes, you maintain control of your tickets until they're sold. Just let us know before the sale is completed.
