Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How does Victory Kay Tickets work?

Simply submit your unused tickets through our "Book Now" form. We handle all pricing, listing, customer service, and transfers across major secondary marketplaces. You receive payment once your tickets sell.

What sports and events do you handle?

We work with tickets from all major sports leagues including MLB, NFL, NBA, NHL, MLS, and premium events. If you have season tickets or individual game tickets you can't use, we can help sell them.

How do you price my tickets?

We use real-time market data, opponent strength, game timing, weather conditions, and historical trends to set optimal prices that maximize your returns.

What marketplaces do you list on?

We list your tickets simultaneously on StubHub, Vivid Seats, SeatGeek, Ticketmaster Resale, and other leading platforms to ensure maximum exposure and the best possible sale price.

How do I get paid?

Once your tickets sell, we send you a full sale summary and ask how you'd like to receive your proceeds. We operate on an 80/20 split — 80% goes directly to you.

What if my tickets don't sell?

There are no upfront costs or fees. If your tickets don't sell, you pay nothing. You maintain control and can use the tickets yourself right up until they're sold.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard security measures and coordinate transfers directly through official team systems to ensure authenticity and security.

Can I still use my tickets after submitting them?

Yes, you maintain control of your tickets until they're sold. If you decide you want to attend the game yourself, just let us know before the sale is completed.
