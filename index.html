Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How does Victory Kay Tickets work?

Simply submit your unused tickets through our "Book Now" form. We handle all pricing, listing, customer service, and transfers across major secondary marketplaces.

What sports and events do you handle?

We work with tickets from all major sports leagues including MLB, NFL, NBA, NHL, MLS, and premium events.

How do you price my tickets?

We use real-time market data, opponent strength, game timing, weather conditions, and historical trends to set optimal prices.

What marketplaces do you list on?

We list simultaneously on StubHub, Vivid Seats, SeatGeek, Ticketmaster Resale, and other leading platforms.

How do I get paid?

Once your tickets sell, we send you a full sale summary. We operate on an 80/20 split — 80% goes directly to you.

What if my tickets don't sell?

There are no upfront costs or fees. If your tickets don't sell, you pay nothing.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard security measures and coordinate transfers directly through official team systems.

Can I still use my tickets after submitting them?

Yes, you maintain control of your tickets until they're sold. Just let us know before the sale is completed.
