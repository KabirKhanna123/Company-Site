📊 Column Guide
Event
The name of the event. Click any row to open the full event analysis page with price history charts and section data.
Cat
Event category — FIFA, NBA, MLB, NFL, NHL, F1, Concert, etc. Use the category filter to narrow your view.
Days
Days until the event. Red = 7 days or less (urgent), Gold = within 30 days, Grey = further out. Sortable.
Date
The event date. Default sort is soonest first. Click the column header to toggle ascending/descending.
Venue
The venue name. Use the venue search bar to filter by location.
Floor
Cheapest listing currently on StubHub for this event. This is the most important pricing reference — your listing needs to beat this to sell first. Sortable.
ATP
Average Ticket Price — the mean price across all active listings. Useful for gauging the midpoint of the market and pricing for margin.
Listings
Total number of active listings on the platform right now. High listings = more competition. Low listings = less supply, often means prices are rising.
Demand
VKT's proprietary Demand Score (1–99). Calculated from days to event, sell-through rate, floor price trend, and listing volume momentum. 60+ = high demand, 35–59 = moderate, below 35 = low. Sortable.
Sold ▼
Sell-through signal — the net change in listings since first tracked. A negative number (e.g. -24) means 24 listings were sold. A positive number means supply is growing. Sortable.
24h Vol
24-hour volume change — % change in listing count over the last 24 hours. Green = listings dropping (selling fast), Red = listings growing (more supply). Sortable.
Alert 🔔
Set a floor price alert. Enter a target floor price and your email — you'll be notified when the floor drops to or below your target. Alerts are saved in your browser.
